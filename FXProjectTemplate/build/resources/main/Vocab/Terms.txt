REFLEXIVE WORDS

How to use me/m', te/t', se/s', nous, vous, se/s'
Marie a réveillé = Marie woke [someone else] up.
Marie se réveille = Marie wakes up (herself).

How to use "y"

1) Replaces a place
Ex: Je vais à Paris → J’y vais (I'm going to paris → I'm going there)
Ex: Il est dans le salon → Il y est (He is in the living room → He is there)

2) It replaces "à" + a thing (NOT a person)
Ex: Il pense à son avenir → Il y pense (He’s thinking about his future → He’s thinking about it.)
Ex: Elle répond à la question → Elle y répond (She answers the question → She answers it.)
